Hi! Drop your girlfriend's photo here and rename it to 'girlfriend.jpg'.
It will be used as the avatar and album art for the Spotify music player!

For the 12 running marquee cards, you can upload EITHER a video OR a photo in this folder!

For each slot (1 to 12), you can choose to upload:
- A photo named: photo1.jpg, photo2.jpg, ... photo12.jpg
- OR a video named: video1.mp4, video2.mp4, ... video12.mp4

How it works:
1. The website attempts to load the MP4 video (`video1.mp4`, etc.) first.
2. If the video is found, it will play silently and loop inline inside the running marquee.
3. If no video is found, the website automatically loads the corresponding JPG photo (`photo1.jpg`, etc.).
4. If neither is found, the website displays a beautiful custom romantic CSS gradient card with handwriting captions!
5. When clicked, any photo or video will pop open inside a gorgeous rose-gold Polaroid lightbox. Videos in the lightbox will play with sound and full user controls!
